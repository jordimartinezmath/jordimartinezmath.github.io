## Teaching

I have experience teaching the fundamental theorem of calculus
\\[ \int_a^b f'(x) \, dx = f(b) - f(a) .\\]
This is related to the isomorphism
$\mathrm{Pic}^0(X) \cong H^0(X, \Omega^1)^\vee / H_1(X, \mathbb Z)$
when $X$ is an algebraic curve.
